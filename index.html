About FGNA

The Federal Gang & Narcotics Agency (FGNA) is a specialized federal division under the Department of State, tasked with neutralizing high-risk gang operations and dismantling narcotics networks across the United States. Our tactical, intelligence, and operations teams are trained to respond with precision, strength, and discretion.

Operational Divisions

Narcotics Enforcement Division

Conducts undercover operations, seizures, and arrests tied to major drug trafficking organizations.

Gang Task Force

Surveils, tracks, and dismantles criminal gangs across urban and rural zones.

Tactical Operations Unit

Leads high-risk raids, hostage rescue, and counter-gang warfare under S.E.R.T protocol.

Cyber & Intelligence Bureau

Intercepts communications, analyzes data, and provides real-time surveillance intel.
